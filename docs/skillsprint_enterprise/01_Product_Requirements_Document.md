# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - SkillSprint

## 1. Product Vision
SkillSprint is a modern, scalable, mobile-first AI-powered Micro-Learning Platform. It blends the engaging, short-form mechanics of TikTok/Instagram Reels with the educational depth of Coursera/Duolingo. The platform focuses on AI, Programming, Data Science, Cloud, and GATE concepts.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Students & Professionals**: Looking to learn new skills in 5-10 minute chunks daily.
- **Instructors**: Looking for a modern platform to publish short-form educational content.
- **Admins**: Managing platform health and user safety.

## 3. Key Features
- **TikTok-Style Feed**: Auto-playing short-form educational videos with infinite scroll, likes, bookmarks, and sharing.
- **AI Tutor**: Course-specific conversational AI capable of generating quizzes, explaining concepts, and answering queries.
- **Gamification**: Streaks, XP, Badges, and Leaderboards.
- **Live Learning**: WebRTC/WebSocket based live classes and Q&A.
- **Offline Mode**: Downloading lessons and syncing progress offline.

## 4. User Roles & RBAC
- **Student**: Consumes content, takes quizzes, interacts with AI.
- **Instructor**: Creates courses, modules, videos, and monitors analytics.
- **Admin**: Content moderation, global analytics, system health.
